Telugu Tots

A quiet little project for families who want their children to grow up with warm memories, gentle play, and a soft connection to where they come from.

No launch countdowns. No noise. Just thoughtful steps, shared slowly.

Everyday Calm

Little hands, quiet stories.

The project began on the floor — soft fabric, wooden toys, and the small rituals that fill a home at the edges of the day. Telugu Tots grows from these ordinary, gentle moments.

Roots & Places

Between here and home.

For many little ones, stories stretch across oceans. We’re exploring ways for children to feel at home in both worlds — curious about where they are, and gently rooted in where they come from.

Journeys & Play

Small roads to somewhere.

Wheels, pages, tiny adventures—nothing loud, nothing rushed. Just objects designed to be held, loved, and remembered long after small hands have outgrown them.
